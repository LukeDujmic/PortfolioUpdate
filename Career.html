My visions for the future!

General Vision

When looking at my career, I envision a path to a career based off of software development, or some field tied into coding. It is something I've seeked ever since my enjoyment in video games and the internet, and something I am currently pursuing at this very moment.

West-Mec

In my home school, West-Mec was consistently brought up and promoted by the school administrators and announcements. My interest to join West-Mec sparked when I discovered a newly developed coding program to attend for my Junior and Senior years of high school. To me it was an unexpected but willfully accepted opportunity. And so my discrete path in pursuing a field in coding finally begun. Beforehand, I had taken other classes that somewhat tied into coding. This was a S.T.E.M class in 8th grade and two Engineering classes in my first two years of high school, which provided very minor but still apparent experiences in coding.

Currently at West-Mec Northeast Campus, I attend a coding class where students are given the opportunity to learn coding languages of HTML, CSS and JavaScript entirely. The class also allows students to become JavaScript certified, which will lead them into getting a job early on without attending college. I do not plan on following this plan of skipping college, but I will take advantage of my knowledge and JavaScript certification.
